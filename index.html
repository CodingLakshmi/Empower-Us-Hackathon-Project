Our Mission

Our goal was to create something to empower women and girls all around the world. Many women don't know that there is help available. They don't know what do do when something terrible happens to do them. We aimed to create something to help women in situations such as domestic violence, rape and child abuse.

What is Empower US?

Empower US is a website created to help women and girls stay safe and learn what to do if something happens to them. By filling out a form, they are taken to a page with helpful advice and prevention tips. They would be able to reach out for help and plan for the future. Empower US also enables victims and survivors of abuse and violence to connect with one another, through our volunteering program and community forum. Using this platform, we can make the world a safer and happier place.
